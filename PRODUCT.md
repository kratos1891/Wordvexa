# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who write prompts, refine AI-assisted text, and need a focused workspace to turn raw ideas or externally generated AI output into useful, natural Spanish content.

## Product Purpose

Wordvexa is a prompt and text-humanization app. It helps users craft better prompts, improve AI-generated text, preserve important expressions, and adjust the tone or regional variant of the result. Success means the user can move from draft to usable output quickly, without fighting the interface.

## Brand Personality

Professional, modern, clear. The interface should feel capable and work-focused, with enough polish to feel trustworthy but not decorative.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-landing-page composition, overloaded visual effects, playful or juvenile styling, and controls that look decorative instead of functional.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the writing workflow direct and visible.
2. Use familiar product UI patterns for navigation, forms, and actions.
3. Make every control visibly affect the current task.
4. Prefer calm clarity over ornament.
5. Preserve user intent when transforming text.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, keyboard-friendly controls, and reduced-motion-safe transitions.
